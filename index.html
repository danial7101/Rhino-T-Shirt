Printing Solutions
Tailored for Every Need

From corporate wear to creative merchandise, Rhino T-Shirt delivers high-quality, professional printing services trusted by both government and private sectors.

Silkscreen Printing

Ideal for bulk printing on t-shirts (short & long sleeve), aprons, non-woven bags, gift items, and cosmetic packaging — cost-effective and long-lasting.

Heat Press

Perfect for personalized prints on mugs, caps, and more. A vibrant option for promotional and gift items with quick turnaround.

Sublimation

Full-color, all-over prints on polyester-based fabrics. Great for sportswear, jerseys, and lightweight apparel.

Embroidery

Professional stitching for caps, towels, windbreakers, and corporate t-shirts — adding texture, durability, and class to your brand.

Custom-Made Apparel

Polo shirts, jerseys, jackets, uniforms, and preschool wear — tailored designs with flexible fabric and sizing options for every group or occasion.

DTF & UV Printing

High-resolution Direct-To-Film (DTF) and UV printing for bold, long-lasting designs on a variety of surfaces — perfect for vibrant, custom detail.
